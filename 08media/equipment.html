Equipment

Investing in the right equipment is crucial for producing professional-quality voice recordings that meet industry standards. A reliable microphone, audio interface,
and a properly treated recording space ensure that your voice sounds clear, crisp, and free of background noise or distortion. Poor audio quality can immediately
disqualify you from jobs, no matter how strong your performance is. By using the right tools, you not only enhance your sound but also demonstrate professionalism and
respect for the production process—making it easier for clients, casting directors, and producers to take you seriously.

Important Terms to Know

Recording Studio
A sound-treated space designed to record clean, high-quality audio with minimal noise or echo.
Microphone
Captures your voice and turns it into an audio signal for recording.
Headphones
Let you hear your recordings clearly and catch mistakes or background noise.
Recording Software
A program used to record, edit, and export your voice work (e.g., Audacity, Reaper).
Audio Interface
Connects your mic to your computer and improves sound quality for recording.

Best Choices For Beginners

Microphones

  • Audio-Technica AT2020 — Affordable, great sound quality for beginners
  • Rode NT1-A — Known for low self-noise and warm tone
  • Blue Yeti (USB) — Easy setup with USB, good for home studios

Audio Interface (if using XLR mics)

  • Focusrite Scarlett 2i2 — Popular, reliable, and beginner-friendly
  • PreSonus AudioBox USB — Affordable with good preamps

Headphones

  • Audio-Technica ATH-M50x — Clear, balanced sound, great for monitoring
  • Sony MDR-7506 — Industry standard for audio monitoring

Accessories

  • Pop Filter — Reduces plosive sounds (P, B)
  • Mic Stand or Boom Arm — Keeps mic stable and adjustable
  • Acoustic Treatment — Foam panels or blankets to reduce echo and room noise

Recording Software (DAW)

  • Audacity (free) — Simple, beginner-friendly
  • Reaper — Affordable, powerful, widely used
  • Adobe Audition — Professional, subscription-based
